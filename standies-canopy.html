Standies are freestanding display units used to showcase promotional messages, branding, or advertisements at events, exhibitions, retail stores, and other locations. They typically feature eye-catching graphics, images, and text to attract attention and convey key messages effectively.

Standies can be customized with your brand logo, colors, and messaging to create a cohesive visual identity that aligns with your brand image. They come in various sizes, shapes, and materials to suit different display needs and environments.

# Our Specialities

  • Design: Create eye-catching designs that reflect your brand identity and attract attention from a distance. Use bold colors, compelling graphics, and concise messaging to communicate your key message clearly and effectively.

  • Size and Shape: Choose the size and shape of your standee or canopy based on your space limitations and visibility requirements. Consider factors such as venue size, audience traffic flow, and display restrictions when determining the dimensions of your promotional materials.

  • Printing: Select a printing method and material that ensures high-quality and durability for your standees and canopies. Choose weather-resistant materials for outdoor use and opt for vibrant colors and crisp graphics to enhance visibility and impact.

  • Assembly:Ensure that your standees and canopies are easy to assemble and transport to different locations. Provide clear instructions or training for staff members responsible for setting up and dismantling the displays to minimize downtime and ensure a professional appearance.

Canopies, also known as pop-up tents or gazebos, are portable shelter structures used for outdoor events, promotions, festivals, fairs, markets, and other outdoor activities. They provide shade, protection from the elements, and a branded space for showcasing products, services, or information.

Overall, standies and canopies are valuable assets for enhancing brand visibility, creating memorable experiences, and maximizing promotional opportunities at events and outdoor venues. With their customizable design options, portability, and versatility, they offer a cost-effective way to make a lasting impression and achieve marketing objectives.
